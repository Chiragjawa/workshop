About the Event

The Wolf of Dalal Street , organized by the ECON club , aims to provide students of TIET with an enthralling experience that enables them to immerse themselves in the captivating world of shares, stocks, and equity. This event offers an exceptional opportunity for participants to gain practical insights into the intricate workings of the stock market, while honing their decision-making skills and financial acumen.
